About Beyond Hunger

Our Mission

We are dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger in our communities by connecting surplus food with those who need it most.

At Beyond Hunger, we believe that no one should go hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. Our platform facilitates the donation and distribution of excess food to individuals and families facing food insecurity.

Founded in 2022, our organization has grown into a community-driven movement of volunteers, donors, and supporters committed to creating a more sustainable and equitable food system.
